E-Commerce · Maintenance Plan · Sports & Outdoors

BasketHub

How a sports equipment e-commerce store scaled from $8k to $31k monthly revenue by fixing what was silently killing their conversions.

The Problem

  • Site running on PHP 7.2 — end of life, full of vulnerabilities
  • Product images not optimized — 8MB files killing load speed
  • No caching — every page rebuilt from scratch on each visit
  • Payment gateway timing out on mobile devices

What We Did

  • PHP upgraded to 8.2 — full compatibility testing

  • Payment gateway reconfigured and tested across devices
  • All 47 crawl errors resolved — sitemap resubmitted

The Results

  • Load speed: 9.3s → 1.6s
  • Mobile checkout completion: 31% → 74%
  • Google ranking improved for 12 product keywords
  • Monthly revenue: $8k → $31k in 90 days

FAQ

Questions We Get All the Time

How long does it take to build a website?
Most projects are delivered in 7–14 business days. The timeline depends on scope: a landing page typically takes 3–5 days, a full business website 7–10 days, and an e-commerce store 10–14 days. We share a clear timeline in your proposal before starting.
Do I need to provide content and images?
Yes — you provide your logo, brand colors, text content, and any specific images you want to use. We provide guidance on exactly what we need. If you don't have professional photos we can source high-quality stock images that match your brand at no extra cost.
Do you show me the design before coding starts?
Always. We design everything in Figma first and share it with you for review and approval. You can request changes at this stage. We only start coding once you are completely happy with the design. This protects both sides and ensures no surprises at the end.
What if I don't like the final result?
We include revision rounds in every project. You review the development build and give feedback — we apply changes until you are satisfied. Since you approved the design in Figma first, the final result is almost always exactly what you expected. We don't deliver and disappear.
Do you work with clients outside Pakistan?
Yes — the majority of our clients are international. We have worked with businesses in the US, UK, Australia, UAE, Canada, Europe, and more. All communication is in English and we adapt to your timezone for calls and meetings. International payments are accepted via bank transfer, Wise, PayPal, and other methods.
Who owns the website after it is delivered?
You do — 100%. Upon final payment you receive all source files, admin credentials, hosting access, and full ownership of everything we built. We don't hold anything back or lock you into ongoing payments to keep your site live.
Can you redesign my existing website instead of building from scratch?
Yes — redesign is one of our most popular services. We review your current site, identify what's hurting your conversions, and rebuild it with a modern design and better performance. The process is the same: Figma mockup first, your approval, then development.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch?
Every project includes a post-launch support period for any bugs or small fixes. For ongoing support beyond that, we offer monthly maintenance plans that cover updates, backups, security monitoring, and priority support. These start from $80/month depending on your site's needs.
